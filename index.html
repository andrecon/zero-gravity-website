Projects

Project Happy School

A voluntary government school makeover project inspired by the concept of Bala (building as a learning aid) which is an innovative way to look at the relationship of a child with the school space. It is about developing the school’s entire physical environment as a learning aid-the inside, the outside, everywhere. At the core it is about maximizing the educational value of built space through visual imagery. It emphasizes on the importance of the physical learning environment, infrastructure and visuals in the overall educational process.

Project Shelter Home Makeover

A project aimed at transforming homeless shelters for women and children into places that inspire behavioral and psychological change through our various mentoring programs and makeover of the physical space, women and children are introduced to a way of thinking that helps to shift their outlook about their lives and possibilities for the future.

Project Happiness

Maximum Happiness to Maximum number of people” We design activities, campaigns and events of kindness that inspire people to give attention, affection and appreciation creating a pandemic of happy people around the world.

Project Hindustan

We adopt a village, make people our family and work together on providing services and solutions to rural problems, empowering local hands to sustain themselves to become a holistically developed community of a model village

Project Earth

Because “There is no Planet B” We initiate campaigns and projects to create awareness leading to action in individuals and communities with respect to making the best use of environmental resources while conserving them at the same time.
